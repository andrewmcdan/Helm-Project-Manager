Definitions

Functional requirement What it does

A feature or behavior the system must provide.

Non-functional requirement How it behaves

Performance, security, usability, and other quality constraints.

Effort categories Activities

Requirements Analysis, Design, Coding, Testing, Project Management.

Risk likelihood vs impact Probability vs damage

Likelihood is how likely the risk is; impact is how severe it is.

Troubleshooting

Cannot sign in Auth

Confirm your account is active and password is correct.

Missing requirement in Effort dropdown Requirement

Ensure the requirement exists and is not archived.

Unable to save effort Validation

Requirement, category, and hours are required and must be valid.

Totals not updating Refresh

Reload the page or check filters for the selected range.
